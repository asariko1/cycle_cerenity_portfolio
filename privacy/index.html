This Privacy Policy explains how Cycle Serenity and Asariko ("we", "our", or "us") handle information when you use the Cycle Serenity mobile application and related website or services. 1. Summary Cycle Serenity is designed with a privacy-first approach. Cycle Serenity does not require an account and does not store your cycle data on external servers. Your data remains on your device. We do not sell your personal data. 2. Information You Enter in the App Cycle Serenity may allow you to enter information such as: - cycle and period dates - symptoms, notes, and daily logs - preferences and app settings - optional profile information such as a name or nickname This information is stored locally on your device as part of the app experience. 3. Subscription and Purchase Information If you purchase premium features, subscription and transaction processing may involve third-party platforms and services, including: - Apple App Store - Google Play - RevenueCat or similar subscription infrastructure We do not receive your full payment card details. Notification content may be visible on your device’s lock screen depending on your device settings. Cycle Serenity includes a setting to control this behavior ("Show detail text on lock screen"), which is disabled by default. You are responsible for managing notification visibility preferences on your device. 5. How We Use Information We use information only as needed to: - provide core app functionality - display your cycle records, logs, and predictions - store your preferences - determine premium access where applicable - improve, maintain, and support the app 6.
